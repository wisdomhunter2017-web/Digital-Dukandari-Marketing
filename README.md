# 🏪 Digital Dukandari - Marketing Scripts

> **"Pakistani Dukaandaar Ki Smart Partner"**

Ye repository Digital Dukandari app ki marketing ke liye tayyar ki gayi hai. Isme video scripts, social media captions, aur content calendar maujood hain.

---

## 📂 Is Repository Mein Kya Hai?

| Folder | Kya Hai? |
| :--- | :--- |
| **scripts/15-second/** | Instagram Reels, TikTok, aur YouTube Shorts ke liye 15-second video scripts. |
| **scripts/30-second/** | Facebook Feed aur Instagram Posts ke liye 30-second video scripts. |
| **scripts/long-form/** | YouTube videos aur detailed ads ke liye 1.5-2 minute ki scripts. |
| **captions/** | Social media posts ke liye ready-made Urdu captions. |

---

## 🎯 Ye Scripts Kis Kaam Aayengi?

Ye scripts **"Digital Dukandari"** app ke liye banaai gayi hain. Har script 3 cheezon par focus karti hai:

1. **Dard (Pain Point):** Dukaandaar ki pareshani (udhaar, stock, munshi).
2. **Hal (Solution):** App ka feature.
3. **Action (CTA):** App download karne ki darkhwast.

---

## 📲 Digital Dukandari App Ke Baare Mein

Digital Dukandari ek Pakistani mobile app hai jo dukaandaron ko unka:

- Sales/Purchases
- Udhaar (Customer Ledger)
- Stock Management
- Staff & Payroll
- Profit/Loss Reports

...sab kuch ek hi jagah, **Urdu aur English** mein, aur **bina internet** ke bhi sambhalne deti hai.

---

## 🚀 Is Repository Ko Kaise Use Karein?

1. **Video Banane Ke Liye:** `scripts/15-second` mein se koi bhi script choose karein. Use CapCut ya any editing app mein daal kar reel banayein.
2. **Caption Ke Liye:** `captions` folder se relevant caption copy karein aur Instagram/Facebook par paste karein.
3. **Schedule Ke Liye:** Har haftay ki content calendar follow karein taake rozana naya content aata rahe.
